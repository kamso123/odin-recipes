Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flat, round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It’s baked in an oven, creating a crispy crust and melty, flavorful combination of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Dough
    • Flour
    • Yeast
    • Olive oil
    • Salt
  • Sauce
    • Tomato sauce
    • Cheese
    • Basil
    • Pepperoni, sausage, vegetables, mushrooms, etc.

Steps

  1. Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a flat, round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It’s baked in an oven, creating a crispy crust and melty, flavorful combination of ingredients.
  2. Prepare the sauce: Combine tomato sauce, olive oil, garlic, oregano, salt, and sugar.
  3. Preheat the oven: Heat to 475°F (245°C).
  4. Shape the dough: Roll out the dough to your desired thickness on a floured surface.
  5. Assemble the pizza: Spread the sauce over the dough, then top with shredded mozzarella, cheese, and your choice of toppings.
  6. Bake: Place the pizza on a baking sheet or pizza stone and bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  7. Serve: Slice and enjoy =)
